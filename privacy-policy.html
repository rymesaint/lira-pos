1. Introduction

Welcome to LIRA POS ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our point-of-sale application and related services. LIRA POS integrates advertising services, including Unity Ads, to provide you with relevant advertisements while using our application.

Important: By using LIRA POS, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account registration information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Business information (business name, address, tax identification)
  • Payment processing information (encrypted payment data)
  • Customer transaction records

2.2 Usage Data

  • Application usage patterns and preferences
  • Transaction history and sales data
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error logs and performance data

2.3 Device Information

  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, Advertising ID, Unity Analytics ID)
  • Operating system and version
  • Device model and manufacturer
  • IP address and location data (when permitted)
  • Network information and connectivity status
  • Unity-specific identifiers for advertising and analytics

3. Unity Advertising Services

3.1 Unity Ads Integration

LIRA POS uses Unity Ads to display advertisements within the application. Unity Ads may collect and process the following information:

  • Advertising ID and Unity Analytics ID for personalized ads
  • Device information and specifications (model, OS, screen size)
  • App usage data and session information for ad targeting
  • Location data (if location services are enabled)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views, completion rates)
  • Network and connectivity information
  • Unity-specific performance and crash data

3.2 Ad Personalization

Unity uses your Advertising ID to show you personalized ads based on your interests and app usage. You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization, or through Unity's privacy settings
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads (turn off)
  • Unity Ads: Visit Unity's privacy portal to manage your advertising preferences
