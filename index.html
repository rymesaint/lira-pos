Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09 September 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our POS (Point of Sale) application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Such as email address, only when you choose to sign up or contact us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and settings used.
  • Storage Data: Data related to sales, inventory, and transactions, stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To enable backup and restore functionality using your Google Drive account.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.

3. Data Storage and Security

All sales and transaction data are stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud backup, your data will be stored securely in your personal Google Drive account. We do not access or share this data without your consent.

4. Advertisements

Our free version may display ads from third-party providers. These providers may use cookies or unique device identifiers to deliver relevant ads. You can remove ads by subscribing to the Premium Plan.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

The app offers premium features through paid subscriptions. Payments are processed securely via the Google Play Store or App Store. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled before the end of the billing cycle. Pricing and terms are shown clearly in the app before purchase.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Google Drive to provide backup and synchronization features. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information to others. Data is only shared when required by law.

8. Your Choices

  • You can use the app fully offline without enabling cloud backup.
  • You may enable or disable Google Drive backup at any time.
  • You may remove ads by upgrading to Premium.
  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or website.
